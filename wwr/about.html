About Our Company

At White Water Rafting Co., we’ve been guiding thrilling river adventures for more than 15 years. Our mission is simple: to create unforgettable outdoor experiences while ensuring safety, respecting nature, and building lasting memories for every guest.

From gentle family-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping Class V rapids, we offer something for everyone. Our team is committed to excellence in training, customer service, and environmental stewardship.
